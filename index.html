Matt Nida

I'm Matt and I live in London.

As a TV producer, I made the BAFTA-winning Joe Lycett vs David Beckham special, Channel 4's festive centrepiece The Big Fat Quiz of the Year, a political mockumentary where I followed Al Murray on the 2015 election trail, Rhod Gilbert's Growing Pains, and a bunch of other things that you probably missed but I'm still proud of them.

Away from telly, I've also produced live comedy shows for Rob Brydon, Lee Mack & David Mitchell and John Robins & Elis James, I've released some records, I co-host a supremely nerdy podcast, and I have a couple of other fun music-related projects too.
